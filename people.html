The People of the Republic of Xemnas


  I. Neighbourhood (Houses)
   a. All the households are on the east side of Xemnas
    1. Every family is assigned to the same type of villa in the same neighbourhood
    2. There are 10 houses in a row
    3. The houses are really modern and filled with the same looking and cozy furniture
     I. Every house will have a garden that has the same types of trees and flowers planted
     II. In every house there is a maid for:
      a. Washing dishes
      b. Cleaning the house
      c. Looking after the kids
      d. Looking to the garden
      e. Cooking food
    4. There is a huge park at the end where the dwellings(houses) finish
     I. This park contains:
      a. Restaurants
      b. Playing area for the kids
      c. A lake
      d. Benches to sit
     II. The park:
      a. Is open 7/24
      b. Is the safest place
      c. In emergency the people will gather here
    5. The families are assigned to their houses according to their jobs
     I. Which is helping the people:
      a. Be together
      b. Work easily after the work time
      c. Transport easily
    6. The houses are illustrated on the picture on the top-right
    7. The houses that every family is assigned is their private place
     I. The families can not go to each others house, they can only meet together in the park
  II. Families:
   a. The families are allowed to make only 2 kids
    1. The gender of the kids don’t matter because the importance of having two kids is making the kids don’t grow up alone and have a partner to help in every situation
     I. The parents are assigned to each other to stop jealousy
   b. Every family member lives in the same dwelling and makes the kids spend time together with their parents often
    1. Also, the family living together helps the children understand they are not alone and they have parents behind them which will help them and care for them
   c. The children’s role in the society is to create a real family, get educated to be assigned to jobs and help the community be a better place with their new ideas
    1. Also, the kids help the family chain go forever
  III. Leadership:
   a. The president is elected by the community
   b. Gender doesn’t matter but the president should fit in the requirements
   c. Every ten years the presidential elections happen
   d. After the president has finished his/her ten years he/she cannot try to be in the election again
   e. The candidate that gives the best speech and gets the most votes from the community
    1. will be the president for 10 years
   f. In order to be the president the candidate should have at least 20 years of experience as a:
    1. Police Officer
    2. Soldier
    3. Teacher
    4. Engineer
    5. Doctor
    6. Lawyer
   g. Every decision that is made Xemnas are made the by the president and the vice presidents that is chosen by the president according to their:
    1. Knowledge
    2. Experience
    3. Capability
