 The Decleration of Independence of the Rebublic of Xemnas


  In order of this document, the Republic of Xemnas would like to request to be considered a nation in of itself. In our republic, we are always striving to be better and because of   this, we are always looking for ways to improve our republic.


  In this strive to be better, the Republic of Xemnas has some qualities that are expected and highly rewarded. We appreciate the values that we preach in our community, such as   loyalty, honesty, discipline, and punctuality of our citizens. These values are upheld and expected from all of our citizens, no matter the circumstances. These values are highly   honored and rewarded as needed. In our community, we are different from many other communities. Here, we do not tolerate lies that mean harm to others. We also expect each   citizen to give back to the community, as each citizen must donate blood once they are of legal age and plant a tree for each birthday.


  The Republic of Xemnas keeps the core elements of republics of today such as incarceration and mandatory education. However, The Republic of Xemnas also goes above and   beyond these core elements, enforcing the no discrimination rules as well as encouraging our citizens to give back to the community by donating blood once they are of legal age   and having to plant a tree on their birthdays.
